What is an AI Powered ChatBot

A chatbot, or chat bot, is a machine/software that has a conversation with humans via text or audio. An AI powered chatbot is a smarter version which uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML) to better understand the intent of the human and provide a more natural, near human-level communication..Now everyone can talk to your business in their favorite messenger. Yantrik chatbots can be deployed on multiple applications like customer website, workplace intranet, facebook messenger, skype and can be used as text chat or voice chat on multiple languages. Our chatbots are intuitive to use and can be implemented into several fields of business such as e-commerce, employee support, entertainment, news, customer support, delivery services or corporate information exchange.

Why ChatBots?

Speed-up processes & Simplify communication...

Human-machine communication has long become a part of our daily life. Smartphones, laptops, and personal assistants are already at our fingertips, ready to respond to our commands. With artificial intelligence (AI) powered chatbots, we can take this communication another step further. At Yantrik our mission is to create chatbots able to disrupt the current status quo and provide even faster processing, faster answers, faster and smarter results.

Eliminate mundane tasks by creating virtual assistants so people can concentrate on things that matter...

We believe that humans are inherently creative and deserve interesting, engaging, and inspiring tasks, that’s why we want to eliminate highly repeatable and programmable tasks from the list of their daily duties. By creating AI based chatbots, we enable professionals to quickly and efficiently solve routine chores so that they can concentrate on things that really require a human touch.
